Contact

Technical difficulties

To report any technical difficulties during participation, please contact our team by email at lookit-tech@mit.edu.

Questions about the studies

If you have any questions or concerns about the studies, please first check our FAQ. With any additional questions, please contact us at lookit@mit.edu.

For researchers

Lookit is available to all developmental researchers, and the code is entirely open-source. To learn more about running your own studies or contributing, please check out the documentation.

Several initial test studies of a prototype Lookit platform were completed in 2015; you can read about the results, and strengths and limitations of the platform, in these papers.
